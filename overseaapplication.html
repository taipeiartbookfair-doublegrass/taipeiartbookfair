Notice
  1. In order to support the art book practices, priority will be given to creators who are currently active. It is recommended to upload your most recent portfolio or work, as well as personal social media links or websites, in the application form for reference during the selection process.
  2. Taipei Art Book Fair encourages participants to develop booth ideas that respond to the theme of each edition. The relevance to the theme will also be considered during the booth selection process and in the evaluation for the Sloppy Award. We welcome everyone to bring their imagination to life!
  3. The organizer will provide appropriate equipment, space, and location based on the selected booth type. No changes can be made after submission, so please choose the booth type that best fits your needs. If you need to make changes, you’ll need to submit a new registration form.
  4. Admission results will be sent via email. Please check both your inbox and junk mail folder. If you are not admitted, no additional notification will be provided.
  5. The exact table size will be confirmed upon acceptance of your application. Detailed information, including the option to purchase additional work permits, will also be provided at that time.
