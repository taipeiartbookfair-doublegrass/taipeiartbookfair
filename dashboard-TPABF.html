品牌簡介 Bio
Born in Tokyo, currently based in Takanabe, in the southern region of Japan. After graduating from the Ceramic Art Course at Kyoto University of the Arts, I expanded my artistic practice to include clay sculpting, along with drawing on paper. In 2022, during an artist residency in Iceland, I created and exhibited a series of ceramic works titled Huldufólk. My recent activities include the solo exhibition Soft Wind (Kinkan Gallery, 2025), as well as starring in and contributing artworks to the short film Yume No Hate Made (Till the End of the World), directed by Natsuka Kusano, part of the omnibus film Performing Hiroshima (2023). At the Taipei Art Book Fair, I will present a new zine featuring recent works created in Takanabe, the town I recently moved to. Through this publication, I hope to share the unique charm and appeal of Takanabe with a wider audience.
