聲明

本表單適用於欲擁有桌位或空地展示／販售出版品、藝術品或裝置的參展者。當我們收到大量申請資料時，主辦單位保留依填表內容篩選、拒絕之權利。


申請時程

申請截止：12月18日（四）24:00

在展會上

主辦單位將依整體展場佈局統一安排攤位位置，為公平起見，平面圖完成前後皆不接受位置偏好申請。

我們將於 2 月提供攤位樣式示意圖。

立面展示因場地空間限制隨機分配，且可能與其他單位共用。



展位類型

錄取參展單位將獲得資格於2026年草率簿中露出個人宣傳一頁，並獲得 2026 草率簿一本。


ㄧ般書攤 ｜5,000 元（含稅）

針對以書本為主要創作／販售的參展單位，申請時需提出三本以上已出版刊物及審查資料。
基礎設備：桌面（120*60cm) 1 個、椅子 2 張、工作證 2 張、草率簿 1 本（含露出一面）


非書（創作）攤｜8,000 元（含稅）

針對以非書類型為主要創作／販售的參展單位。
基礎設備：桌面（120*60cm) 1 個、椅子 2 張、工作證 2 張、草率簿 1 本（含露出一面）


裝置攤｜10,000 元（含稅）

針對大型裝置或空間創作為主的參展單位。
基礎設備：1.5M*1.5M空地、工作證 2 張、草率簿 1 本（含露出一面）


策展攤｜50,000 元（含稅）

針對有大量作品展示需求的參展單位。
基礎設備：3M*3M空間、桌面（120*60cm) 2 個、椅子 4 張、工作證 6 張、草率簿 1 本（含露出一面）


食物酒水攤｜13,000 元（含稅）

針對以食物或飲品為主要販售類型的單位。
基礎設備：桌面（180*60cm) 1 個、椅子 2 張、工作證 2 張、草率簿 1 本（含露出一面）

*每個單位在繳費階段最多可加購 1 張工作證（含入場憑證），以「天」為計算單位。因場地空間有限，若攤位需要超過指定數量之工作人員，或需短暫輪班，可於購票階段購買「親友票」。

About the participation

This form is intended for exhibitors who wish to apply for a tabletop booth or floor space to display or sell publications, artworks, products, or installations.

When receiving a large number of applications, the organizer reserves the right to review, select, or decline submissions based on the information provided.


Deadline

Application Deadline: December 18 (Thu) 24:00
Notification of Results: Before December 25 (Thu) 18:00
    • Accepted applicants must complete payment by January 1 (Thu.) 24:00


At the Fair

Booth locations will be arranged by the organizer according to overall venue planning.

To ensure fairness, we do not accept requests for booth location preferences before or after the floor plan is finalized.

Booth style mockups will be provided in February.

Due to venue limitations, vertical display spaces will be allocated randomly and may be shared with other exhibitors.


Booth Types

All accepted exhibitors will receive one promotional page in the TPABF 2026 Catalog (草率簿) and one complimentary catalog.


- Regular Book Booth NTD$5,000 incl. tax

For exhibitors whose primary practice or sales focus on books.
Applicants must submit at least three previously published titles and relevant review materials.
Includes: 120 × 60 cm table ×1, chairs ×2, passes ×2, TPABF Catalog ×1 (one page featured)


- Regular Non-Book Booth NTD$8,000 incl. tax

For exhibitors whose main works or products are non-book items.
Includes: 120 × 60 cm table ×1, chairs ×2, passes ×2, TPABF Catalog ×1 (one page featured)


- Installation Booth NTD$10,000 incl. tax

For exhibitors presenting large-scale installations or spatial works.
Includes: 1.5 m × 1.5 m space, passes ×2, TPABF Catalog ×1 (one page featured)


- Curation Booth   NTD$50,000 incl. tax

For exhibitors who need a larger space to display a significant amount of work.
Includes: 3m*3m space, 120 × 60 cm table ×2, chairs ×4, passes ×6, TPABF Catalog ×1 (one page featured)
